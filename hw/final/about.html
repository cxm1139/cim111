About Us

Cable Boy was founded in January 2018 by Miles Rosengard, a sophomore studying Accounting and Finance at the University of Miami. Miles spends most of his free time on his apparel line. He specializes in what he calls “graphic hats” which feature his own designed and hand-made embroidery.

Our store offers custom embroidered designs and garment customization as well.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
